Rosemount is a community-first social network built on the open ActivityPub protocol. Connect with communities that matter to you, share with your neighbors, and own your digital presence — without algorithmic manipulation or ads.

COMMUNITIES
Organize around shared interests with their own feeds, events, voice rooms, and member spaces. Create private invite-only communities or open ones for anyone to join.

OPEN FEDERATION
Follow and interact with people across Mastodon, Pixelfed, and thousands of other ActivityPub servers. Your social graph belongs to you, not a corporation.

PRIVATE MESSAGING
End-to-end encrypted direct messages powered by the Signal Protocol. Only you and the recipient can read them — not even Rosemount can.

VOICE ROOMS
Drop into live audio rooms with your community. Raise your hand to speak, or just listen. No scheduling needed.

EVENTS
Organize meetups, classes, and gatherings with RSVP tracking, attendee lists, and integrated maps. Syncs with the fediverse via ActivityPub Event.

STORIES
Share 24-hour ephemeral moments with your community. React with emoji. Stories disappear automatically.

PHOTOS
A beautiful, chronological photo timeline powered by Pixelfed. No algorithmic reordering — just photos from people you follow.

POLLS
Get community input with expiring polls. Single or multiple choice, closes automatically.

EMOJI REACTIONS
React beyond the Like button. Full emoji reaction support compatible with Mastodon-Glitch and Pleroma.

LOCATION SHARING
Share your approximate location with a community when you choose to. Grid-snapped to ~100m for privacy. Auto-expires.

OFFLINE MODE
Read your feed without a connection. Posts you like, boosts, and follows queue up and sync automatically when you're back online.

SCHEDULED POSTS
Write now, publish later. Schedule posts hours or days in advance.

YOUR DATA
Rosemount is open source. Your data lives on servers you or your community controls. You can move between servers at any time.

PRIVACY FIRST
No ads. No tracking. No selling your data. Location is grid-snapped. E2E encryption for DMs. Full privacy nutrition label.
